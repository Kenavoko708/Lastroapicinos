FAQ's

you work on all makes and models of vehicles?

Yes, we have experience working on a wide range of vehicle makes and models, including domestic and import cars, bakkies, SUVs, and more. We stay up to date with the latest automotive technologies and have access to diagnostic equipment to effectively diagnose and repair various vehicles.

Can I bring my vehicle in for diagnostics without committing to a repair?

Absolutely! If you're experiencing issues with your vehicle and are unsure of the cause, we offer diagnostic services to identify the problem. Our technicians will provide you with a diagnosis and recommended repairs, but you are under no obligation to proceed with the repairs.

Do you offer towing services in case my car breaks down?

While we don't offer towing services directly, we can arrange towing for you with reputable towing companies in the area.
